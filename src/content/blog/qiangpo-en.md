### Rituals in Chaos

For an "unshaped woman," rituals are not cages; they are the scaffolding that keeps the vessel from collapsing. What society labels as OCD, I perceive as a meticulous dialogue with my environment. 

In the laboratory, this translates to an uncompromising precision with micropipettes and data points. In life, it manifests as a rhythmic necessity to align my internal world with the external noise of Shanghai.

### Finding the Rhythm

The "compulsion" is actually a search for resonance. When every object and every word is in its rightful place, the spirit can finally detach and observe. It is the silence before the symphony.
